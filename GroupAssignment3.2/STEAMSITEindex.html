Science

 

Parents can encourage learning science in three simple ways:

 

1. Ask questions

2. Look for patterns 

3. Communicate ideas

Technology

Parents can encourage learning technology in three simple ways:

 

1. Study how technology has affected society

2. Conducting research online 

3. How to collaborate with others to solve problems.

Engineering

 

Parents can encourage learning engineering in three simple ways:

 

1. Introducing problems that need to be solved

2. Using materials and tools to prototype

3. Develop a process that identifies the goals for discovering solutions

Arts

Parents can encourage learning arts in three simple ways:

 

1. Discover how to create new worlds

2. Convey meaning and emotion through color and rendering

3. And how to look at the natural world for inspiration

Math

Parents can encourage learning math in three simple ways:

 

1. Explore applications across other fields

2. Analyze and use functions to build

3. Learn the rules of probability to solve problems
